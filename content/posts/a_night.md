---
title: "A Night"
date: "2016-02-05"
tags: ['night', 'hope', 'poetry']
---
    A dream, a sound
    A wait for all we could be

    Turning in rounds
    To an endless serenity

    Fading, descending
    Parallels drawn apart

    Too few are the lights
    Too soon are the nights

    Dawning desires and the fear of fall
    End of the line

    The morning walk
