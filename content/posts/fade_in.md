---
title: "Fade In"
date: "2016-02-15"
tags: ['fade', 'alive', 'poetry']
---
    Fade into to you...
    I am fading too soon...
    I am waiting for you...
    Turning still...
    I am leaning in...
    I am alive....

    Farther and deeper into a dream...
    You've become more alive...

    Than what could be seen.
